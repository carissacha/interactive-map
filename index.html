Welcome to My Interactive Map

This project has been a reflection on the fluid nature of identity and the way places, memories, and experiences shape who we are. Through mapping the connections between Hong Kong and the U.S., I have explored what it means to belong to two cultures, to feel both at home and distant at the same time. The process of creating this interactive piece challenged me in unexpected ways, pushing me to merge storytelling with technology, creativity with logic. It was frustrating at times, but ultimately rewarding, reinforcing that identity, like this project, is something that evolves through trial and error. In the end, home is not a fixed location but a collection of moments, and this project is a way of piecing them together.
